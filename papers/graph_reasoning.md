# Graph-Enhanced Reasoning

## Overview

Graph-enhanced reasoning combines the relational structure of graphs with the generative and reasoning capabilities of large language models (LLMs). By leveraging explicit entity relationships, graph-based retrieval, and structured reasoning paths, these approaches improve interpretability, factual grounding, and multi-hop reasoning.

## Research Directions

* Graph Retrieval-Augmented Generation (GraphRAG)
* Graph-guided prompting
* Knowledge-grounded reasoning
* Graph-based chain-of-thought reasoning
* Multi-hop reasoning over knowledge graphs
* Graph-enhanced retrieval systems
